field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><h3>OEO Training Topics</h3><p>OEO offers in-person training on matters related to equal opportunity and discrimination. These are the training topics we offer:</p><p><strong>Title IX / Preventing Sexual Misconduct</strong><br />Covers the various forms of sexual misconduct (including sexual harassment), the obligation to report, bystander intervention and the university’s response</p><p><strong>General EEO Principles</strong><br />Covers various forms of discrimination (including racial, gender-related, age, religion), prevention tactics and acceptable methods of response</p><p><strong>EEO Principles for Supervisors</strong><br />Covers various forms of discrimination (including racial, gender-related, age, religious), prevention tactics and acceptable methods of response from the supervisor’s perspective</p><p><strong>Search Committee Training</strong><br />Covers the issues of equity and diversity that must be considered in recruitment of new employees, including writing a job description, posting a job announcement, reviewing applicants and interviewing candidates</p><hr /><h3>Request a training</h3><p>Click the button below to fill out the training request form:</p><button style="height:50px;width:200px" onclick="window.location.href='/office-equal-opportunity-training-request-form'"><h6>OEO Training Request Form</h6></button><p>&nbsp;</p><hr /><h3>Give us your feedback</h3><p>If you have already participated in an OEO training, we would love to hear from you. Your feedback helps us improve the material and better serve UNT. Click the button below to fill out the OEO Training Evaluation form:</p><button style="height:50px;width:200px" onclick="window.location.href='https://unt.az1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_0Hx8S2HZLZ3tei9'"><h6>OEO Training Evaluation</h6></button><p>&nbsp;</p><hr /><h3>Equal Opportunity Online Compliance Training</h3><p>OEO offers Title IX/Sexual Misconduct training, and Anti-Discrimination training to university employees through online modules. <em>If you received an email from OEO assigning you to an online/electronic training</em>, please click the button below to log in:</p><button style="height:50px;width:200px" onclick="window.location.href='https://saml-unt.unt.edu/idp/profile/SAML2/Unsolicited/SSO?providerId=https://training.wecomply.com/wc2'"><h6>EO Online Compliance Training</h6></button><p>&nbsp;</p><p><em>Please note that you will not be able to log in to the online training if you have not been assigned to an online course.<br /><strong>You will receive an email from OEO when you are assigned</strong>.</em></p><p>If you have any questions or concerns, please contact our office at <a href="mailto:oeo@unt.edu">oeo@unt.edu</a> or
